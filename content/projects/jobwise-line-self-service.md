context: >-
  Jobwise runs a working-holiday programme serving more than 500 students per
  year. Student support historically ran through one-to-one manual replies and
  individual meetings with staff.
problem: >-
  Every common question consumed staff time in a 1:1 reply. Support volume
  scaled linearly with student numbers, capping the programme's growth and
  burying the team in repetitive work.
solution:
  - Architected a LINE Official Account self-service system with a rich-menu navigation flow that routes common questions to 30-second video answers.
  - Specified content requirements and directed staff production of a trend-style (TikTok/Reels-format) video library, keeping a personable, on-brand tone across a fully standardised support flow.
  - Designed a two-tier escalation path routing unresolved queries to group conference sessions — cutting 1:1 meeting volume while preserving human support for complex cases.
  - Partnered with Crescendo Lab, Taiwan's LINE Official Account Gold-tier technical partner, for the backstage system, while owning the business case, system architecture, content specification and staff coordination end-to-end.
